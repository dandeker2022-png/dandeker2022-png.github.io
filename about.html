About - TSWRDC (W) Nirmal

Telangana Social Welfare Residential Degree College, Nirmal was established on 16-08-2016. It is a specialized residential college for women, affiliated to Kakatiya University and functioning under Telangana Social Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society (TSWREIS).

The college provides undergraduate education to marginalized women with structured reservations: 75% Scheduled Caste, 12.5% Backward Caste, 5% Scheduled Tribe, 2.5% Other Caste, and 5% Sports & Minority. It is one among the 30 degree colleges established by TSWREIS across Telangana.

Located in the heart of Nirmal town in a serene and green environment, the college houses around 600 students with 32 experienced faculty members, 1 Health Supervisor, 1 Physical Director, and 20 support staff.

The college currently offers 7 Undergraduate programmes:

  • BA – History, Economics, Political Science
  • B.Com – General
  • B.Com – Computers
  • B.Sc – Botany, Zoology, Chemistry
  • B.Sc – Microbiology, Zoology, Chemistry
  • B.Sc – Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
  • B.Sc – Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science

The campus is equipped with Computer, Chemistry, Physics, Botany, Zoology, and Microbiology laboratories along with a Seminar Hall.

Vision

To build magnificent and value-based educational institutions that provide high-quality holistic education to marginalized women, on par with the best institutions in the country.

TSWRDC (W) Nirmal provides a caring and nurturing environment where students blossom into confident young women ready to face the world. The institution promotes mutual trust, teamwork, knowledge sharing, social responsibility, and respect for individual dignity.

Mission

To promote curricular and extracurricular excellence beyond the classroom and help marginalized students realize their full potential in every sphere of life.

  • Provide inclusive and high-quality educational programs.
  • Promote ethical values, responsibility, and social accountability.
  • Encourage national development with strong professional ethics.
  • Instill values of love, compassion, equality, and justice.
  • Mentor students to become competent, confident, and responsible citizens.

Examination Committee

The Examination Committee conducts Internal and External Assessments as per university notifications and ordinances. It publishes results, awards certificates, and maintains examination records systematically.

Career Guidance Cell (CGC)

The CGC provides guidance for higher education and national-level entrance exams such as CPGET, NIMCET, ICET, CAT, HCU, Azim Premji University, EFLU and more. Special free coaching is provided for PG aspirants including General Aptitude, Reasoning, English Grammar, and Quantitative Aptitude.

Alumni Relations (DAR)

The Alumni Relations Department fosters lifelong engagement with graduates and strengthens the alumni network of TSWRDC (W) Nirmal.

Placement Cell

The Placement Cell organizes campus drives, competitive exam coaching, and career-oriented programs. Placement Cell, CGC, and DAR committees work collectively for the bright future of students.

Sports

Physical Education is an integral part of student development. Daily physical activities are conducted, and students participate in Inter-Collegiate, University, District, State, and National level tournaments.

Health Centre

Student welfare is a priority. The Health Wing (PANACEA) ensures 24x7 care through regular health check-ups, hemoglobin monitoring, and specialized diet for anemic students. Health records are updated daily.

Ethics & Social Responsibility

The College follows strong professional ethics emphasizing integrity, accountability, inclusiveness, and sustainability. Through mentoring and outreach programs, students are guided to become socially responsible citizens contributing towards nation building and women empowerment.
