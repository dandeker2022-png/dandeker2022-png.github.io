About TGSWRDC (W) Nirmal

Telangana Social Welfare Residential Degree College, Nirmal has been established on 16-08-2016. It is a specialized college for women affiliated to Kakatiya University under Telangana Social Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society.

The college provides undergraduate education to marginalized women with structured reservations: 75% SC, 12.5% BC, 5% ST, 2.5% OC and 5% Sports & Minority.

Programmes Offered

  • BA – History, Economics, Political Science
  • B.Com – General
  • B.Com – Computers
  • B.Sc – Botany, Zoology, Chemistry
  • B.Sc – Microbiology, Zoology, Chemistry
  • B.Sc – Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
  • B.Sc – Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science

The college is equipped with Computer, Chemistry, Physics, Botany, Zoology, and Microbiology laboratories and a Seminar Hall.

A spacious library with academic and competitive books is available. English and Telugu newspapers are provided daily.
