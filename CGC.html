Career Guidance Cell (CGC)

The Career Guidance Cell (CGC) of TSWRDC Nirmal was established to guide students in enhancing their knowledge in their chosen field of study. It provides counseling on various courses and encourages students to pursue higher education aligned with their skills, aptitude, and career aspirations. The Cell prepares students for lifelong learning and helps them excel in their respective areas of expertise.

Vision

To prepare students for a lifelong commitment to learning, enhance their knowledge and career prospects, and transform them into productive global citizens.

Mission

To provide exposure and guidance for students to pursue higher studies in India and abroad.

CGC Team

  • The Principal acts as the Convener and supervises CGC activities.
  • The Coordinator monitors and tracks CGC programs.
  • Three Counselors (from different departments) guide students.

The team acts as facilitators in helping students fulfill their academic and career goals.

CGC Activities

The CGC team identifies students’ potential and interests in Higher Education, Employment, and Entrepreneurship. Students are guided and made aware of national and international opportunities.

Higher Education

  • Placement of final-year students in top universities.
  • Coaching and mock tests for entrance examinations such as:
    • CPGET – Common Post Graduate Entrance Test
    • JAM – Joint Admission Test for M.Sc (IITs & IISc)
    • JEST – Joint Entrance Screening Test
    • TISS – Tata Institute of Social Sciences
    • TIFR – Tata Institute of Fundamental Research
    • APU – Azim Premji University
    • CUET & HCU Entrance Exams
    • CAT – Common Admission Test (MBA)
    • ICET – Integrated Common Entrance Test
    • NIMCET – MCA in NITs
    • Central & Deemed Universities (BHU, Pondicherry University, Tezpur University etc.)

Employment

  • Focus on financial independence of students.
  • Conduct Orientation Programs, Motivational Classes, Personality Development Sessions, Seminars & Workshops.
  • Encourage careers in Civil Services, Group Exams, Banking, MNCs & IT Sector.

Entrepreneurship

  • Develop leadership and intuitive skills through debates, seminars, group discussions and expert talks.
  • Encourage start-ups and small-scale businesses.

Special Programs & Exams

  • SURF – Summer Undergraduate Research Program
  • CCIP – Community College Initiative Program
  • ICE – International Cultural Exchange (AIESEC)
  • Public Policy Exam
  • Tool Designing Training
  • Radio Jockey Training
  • Commercial Pilot Training

Screening & Advanced Training Camps

  • Data Science Training
  • CAT Coaching
  • Civil Services Training
  • CHSS Training
